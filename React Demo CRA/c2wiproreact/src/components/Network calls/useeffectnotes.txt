useEffect:

 
1. called after ALL the render

useEffect(()=>{
        console.log("inside useeffect")
    })


2. executed oly once after initial render , 
wont be executed for re renders
useEffect(()=>{
        console.log("inside useeffect")
    },[])


3.executed every time after the dependency changes , 
wont be executed for re renders
useEffect(()=>{
        console.log("inside useeffect")
    },[somedep(can be state or props)])
